About LetsCinema

Since 2019, LetsCinema has grown into a real-time signal for Indian cinema — especially the South — trusted by fans, trade and press.

Who we are

LetsCinema began in January 2019 and today reaches a community of nearly 500K followers on X alone. Based in Mumbai, we cover Kollywood, Tollywood, Mollywood, Sandalwood, and the Hindi belt.

What we cover

Announcements, casting, trailers, songs, certifications, festival picks, theatre pulses, and realistic box-office trends paired with context.

Why people follow

Speed and accuracy. You get story, status, and sentiment in seconds — from #DiesIrae horror highs to #JanaNayagan countdowns and Anirudh’s new tracks.
